How to play

Move with ←↑↓→ or WASD — on phone, swipe. Matching covers merge into the next era. Chain merges on consecutive moves to build a combo multiplier for bonus points. Reach a cover for the first time to unlock its photocard forever. Daily Challenge deals the same tile sequence to everyone each day — no undo, pure skill.

Hold the board (or H) to peek era names. U undo · R restart · S share. Your game auto-saves.
