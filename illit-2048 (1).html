Slide covers, merge matching eras, reach the full-group card.

How to play

Move with ←↑↓→ or WASD — on phone, swipe the board. When two tiles show the same ILLIT cover, they merge into the next era and the tile value doubles. Every tile keeps its point value in the corner. Climb from SUPER REAL ME (2) all the way to the full-group ILLIT card (2048). Board jams with no moves left → game over.
